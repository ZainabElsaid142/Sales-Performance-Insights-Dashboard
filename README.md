## 💡 Key Features & Analysis
The dashboard is meticulously crafted to cover critical business pillars:

* **📈 KPI Executive Summary:** Four interactive cards providing an instant health check:
    * **Total Revenue:** Tracking financial growth.
    * **Coupon Usage Rate:** Measuring the efficiency of marketing promos.
    * **Average Items Per Cart:** Understanding customer buying patterns.
    * **Total Items Sold:** Volume performance metrics.

* **📅 Trend Analysis:** A sophisticated **Line Chart** illustrating "Average Order Value by Quarter," identifying seasonal shifts and growth trends over time.

* **🎯 Performance Optimization:** 
    * **Revenue by Acquisition Channel:** Analyzing which platforms (Instagram, Email, Google, etc.) drive the most value.
    * **Product Sales Ranking:** Identifying top-performing products to optimize inventory management.

* **⚙️ Operational Efficiency:** A professional **Donut Chart** offering an "Operational Status Overview," visualizing the full lifecycle of orders from "Pending" to "Delivered" or "Cancelled."

## 🎛️ Interactivity
This dashboard is not just static; it is highly dynamic. Users can perform "Deep-Dive" analysis using:
* **Referral Source Slicer:** Filter results by channel.
* **Payment Method Slicer:** Analyze payment trends.
* **Year Slicer:** Compare yearly performance instantly.
